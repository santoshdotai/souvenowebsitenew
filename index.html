How the automation works

The MVP is designed around one practical workflow: buyer enquiry comes in, Souveno helps the team produce a clean PDF quote and track the next action.

1

Buyer sends enquiry

A customer messages on WhatsApp: product, quantity, size, location, or delivery question.

2

Souveno captures it

The enquiry appears in a shared inbox with tags, owner, source, and workflow status.

3

Catalog price is pulled

Relevant product, SKU, customer rate, tax, and delivery details are selected from your catalog.

4

Quote PDF is generated

The team reviews the auto-built quote and creates a clean PDF ready for WhatsApp.

5

Follow-up is tracked

Souveno records Quoted, Follow-up, Ordered, or Lost so deals do not die in chat.
